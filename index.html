About Artist

WINHART is a pop and electronic music artist project created by Indonesian musician Andy Hartanto.

Combining emotional pop songwriting with modern electronic production, WINHART explores themes of memories, relationships and human connection through melodic hooks and atmospheric sound design.

Releasing music independently, WINHART has begun building an audience through digital platforms and social media, with several songs gaining thousands of views on YouTube.

The project focuses on developing original music while collaborating with independent media platforms, radio stations and music curators.

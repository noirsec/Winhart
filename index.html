About Artist

WINHART is a pop and electronic music project created by Indonesian artist Andy Hartanto.

Blending modern pop songwriting with atmospheric electronic production, WINHART explores emotional storytelling through melodic hooks, cinematic textures and contemporary EDM influences.

Releasing music independently, WINHART has begun building an audience through digital platforms and independent radio promotion. The project focuses on creating immersive pop-driven electronic music designed for both streaming listeners and radio audiences.

WINHART is currently developing new releases while collaborating with media platforms, radio stations and independent curators.
